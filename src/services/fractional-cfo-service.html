A fractional CFO, often engaged by startups and small businesses on a contractual basis, serves to provide specialized knowledge in strategic financial planning and manage the company's financial operations and cash flow. Unlike full-time CFOs who are employed exclusively by a single firm, fractional CFOs typically work with multiple startups.
